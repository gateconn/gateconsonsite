Why gatecon?

Ownership

gatecon gives gamers actual ownership of in-game assets via blockchain, represented as Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs).

Interoperability

Assets in Web3 gaming can be used across different games as they exist on the blockchain, not within a specific game.

Transparency

Thanks to blockchain's immutable nature, all transactions and actions in Web3 gaming are publicly verifiable, helping to prevent cheating or fraud.

No gas-fee

You will not pay gas fees during your transactions by playing games offchain.

Gamer-driven economy

With gatecon gamers can trade, sell, or lease their in-game assets, creating a dynamic gaming experience and the potential for real-world income.

Social

You will be able to create communities that produce content about games through interaction with other gamers.
